Future Development

Petitions can be added so that people can make a difference right from the app

Additional data sets and statistics can be added to make the visualizer more comprehensive and useful

This app is currently a website, but could be extended to a mobile app in the future

Adding laws to different parts of the app will allow for users to be more informed on the political nature of nitrate concentrations in their area

Comparisons between the United States' subsidized nitrogenous fertilizer use and that of countries with more efficient farming practices would best be explored in the context of a scientific paper. However, once such a paper is written, its conclusions and charts would be a welcome part of this app
